Keeping your own archive safe

Curio stores your diary on your device and nowhere else. That is the point, and it is also the risk. If you lose the device, wipe the browser's data, or uninstall the app without a Recovery Kit, the archive is gone and cannot be recovered by anyone — including us. Keeping a current Recovery Kit is your responsibility.

Your passphrase

Recovery Kits are encrypted with a key derived from a passphrase you choose. We never see it, never store it, and cannot reset it. A forgotten passphrase means an unreadable kit.

Recording other people

This one matters

Curio can record meetings and conversations. In many countries recording another person requires their consent, and in some it requires everyone's. Curio asks you to confirm the room has been told before it will start, but the legal responsibility for that recording is yours, not ours. Please know the law where you are.
